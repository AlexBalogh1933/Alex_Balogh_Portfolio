  Achievement Parkour Map. This map is based on all 110 (As of version 1.20) achievements in Minecraft Java Edition. There are about 100 different levels that show off the achievement. Some even will be awarded to you when you complete the level. Right now I am about 75% complete with building all the levels. Each level is hand built to symbolize the achievements. Some of them are more literal while others hint at what achievement it is. At the time of adding this to my portfolio I have spent 1.5 days working on this world.
