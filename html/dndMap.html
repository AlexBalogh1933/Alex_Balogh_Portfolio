  DnD Map: Enchantia. When I wanted to start building my own DnD Campaign I looked through many different world-building applications. Most of them did not have the specific features that I wanted. Then I decided why don't I build it into Minecraft? Minecraft is a place where can control the world and make it my own. So I started building my location in Minecraft. I started with a drawing of the map I wanted to create. Then built the layout in Minecraft then filled it in to make a large area where I could build many different cities. So far I have three cities drawn out and ready to be filled with houses. One city has a main hall built. I also have another area complete with houses and infrastructure.
