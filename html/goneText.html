  Gone is a game that I have been working on for a while. I started it while I was in high school with a group called Explorers Post 891. Explorers Posts are a career-focused group that is adjacent to Boy Scouts. This specific one was for programming and coding. When I was starting to learn C, I started learning how to make games so I started building it in C. After some time, I moved the game over to Python because I was more used to it and it was a more relevant coding language. Gone is a puzzle story game where you explore a world where everyone in it has seemed to disappear. Your job is to go around the world and see if you can figure out where everyone has gone. You explore the world finding clues and solving puzzles. So far I have gotten a couple of rooms and some prompts when entering the different rooms.
