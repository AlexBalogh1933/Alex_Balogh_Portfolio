  Welcome, My name is Alex Balogh. I recently graduated from the University of Cincinnati with a Bachelor of science degree. My major is in Information Technology with 3 tracks; Game Development, Software Development, and Cybersecurity. I grew up in Hilliard, Ohio which is to the west of Columbus, Ohio. I decided to go to the University of Cincinnati because I really liked the campus and the Co-op Program.

  I have loved video games and the process of them being made. One game that has stood out for me is Minecraft. I have been playing it since it was released back in 2010. It was the first game that I played. It turned me into a gamer. It is a game that I can always come back to and play something new. Minecraft was the main reason why I can call myself a gamer. My dream is to create video games that create the next generation of gamers. Minecraft was mine so I want to make games that bring so much join as I had when I first started playing Minecraft. After a while, I started gaining more games in my library. Now I play a wide variety of games but still play Minecraft whenever I can. It is still one of my favorite games. My favorite games are Minecraft, The Sims 4, Roblox, Ark: Survival Evolved, and Forza 5. I have so many other games that I love but those are the games I have been playing recently.

  I have designed a couple of games and made a couple of custom worlds in Minecraft. Those are in the other tabs. I have 1 text-based game I am working on called Gone. Then I started to create that text-based game into a 2D top-down game. I also have a couple of game ideas that I am still working on behind the scenes and still planning. For the Minecraft worlds/maps, I have made a custom world that I am going to use as a map for a Dungeons and Dragons campaign. The other Minecraft map is a parkour course going through all the achievements in the game. Each level is based on the game achievement. More information on these projects will be in other tabs.
