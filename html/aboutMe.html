  Welcome, My name is Alex Balogh. I recently graduated from the University of Cincinnati with a Bachelor of science degree. My major is in Information Technology with 3 tracks; Game Development, Software Development, and Cybersecurity. I grew up in Hilliard, Ohio which is to the west of Columbus, Ohio. I decided to go to the University of Cincinnati because I really liked the campus and the Co-op Program.

  I became a gamer when I first started playing Minecraft. Years later I figured out that I want to make video games. Ever since playing Minecraft I had a dream to create video games that would bring in the next generation of video gamers. I want to be apart of something that bring people to the gaming community. I felt that if I could make video games then I could make a game that brings people to video games like Minecraft did for me. Now I play a wide variety of games but still play Minecraft whenever I can. It is still one of my favorite games. My favorite games are Minecraft, The Sims 4, Roblox, Ark: Survival Evolved, and Forza 5. I have so many other games that I love but those are the games I have been playing recently.

  I have designed a couple of games and made a couple of custom worlds in Minecraft. Enchantia and an Achievement Parkour course. Enchantia is a DnD campaign I am creating and wanted to build a map and world so that I can visualize where things are. The Achievemnt Parkour course is a map that I have been building where each level is a different achievement in Minecraft. These are explained more on another page under Minecraft Specific. I have also been working on a text based game called Gone. I have started building it as a 2D top down game as well. These are also talked about more indepth under the Created Games section. I also have built a couple websites. One is a Sims 4 guide that has a lot of information about things you can do like cheat codes, easter eggs, job rank ups, and some other things.
